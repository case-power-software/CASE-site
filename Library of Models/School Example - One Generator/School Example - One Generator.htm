School Example - One Generator

The model contains two nodes, one generators the rated voltage of which is 110 kV, two lines, and external system which is used to model stiff power grid. Single line diagram with power flow calculation results is given in the next figure.


For transient stability calculation, three phase short circuit is simulated on the one of the lines between nodes Node 1 and External. Fault happens at the time t=1 s and is cleared at the time t=1.15 s.
