Twenty Node Model

The model includes transmission and distribution voltage levels from 400 kV down to 10 kV. It comprises the following power system elements:

  1. Twenty nodes - five 400 kV, seven 110 kV, one 35 kV, two 15.75 kV, one 10.5 kV and four 10 kV.
  2. Three synchronous generating units - two hydro units with salientâ€‘pole rotors and one thermal unit with a cylindrical rotor;
  3. One static generator - used to model solar power plant;
  4. Six transformers - one three winding and five two winding;
  5. Fourteen lines - five 400 kV, six 110 kV, and three 10 kV;
  6. Seven loads [MW/Mvar] - 300/100, 240/80, 150/50/, 90/30, 60/20, 30/10, 1.5/0.5;
  7. External system which is system swing machine.
