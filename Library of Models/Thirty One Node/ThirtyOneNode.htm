Thirty One Node Model

The simulation model includes transmission and distribution voltage levels from 400 kV down to 10 kV. It comprises the following power system elements:

  1. Thirty one nodes - five 400 kV, ten 110 kV, one 35 kV, two 15.75 kV, one 10.5 kV, nine 10 kV, and three 0.69 kV.
  2. Three synchronous generating units - two hydro units with salientâ€‘pole rotors and one thermal unit with a cylindrical rotor;
  3. One static generator - used to model solar power plant;
  4. Three doubly-fed induction generators (DFIG) - used to model wind power plant;
  5. Six transformers - one three winding and five two winding;
  6. Twenty lines - five 400 kV, nine 110 kV, and six 10 kV;
  7. Eleven loads [MW/Mvar] - 300/100, 240/80, 150/50/, 90/30, 60/20, 30/10, 15/5, 15/5, 12/4, 1.5/0.5, 1.0/0.5;
  8. External system which is system swing machine.
