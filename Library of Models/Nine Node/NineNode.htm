Nine Node Model

The model contains:

  1. Nine nodes - four 400 kV, two 110 kV, two 15.75 kV, and one 10.5 kV.
  2. three synchronous generating units - two hydro units with salientâ€‘pole rotors and one thermal unit with a cylindrical rotor;
  3. Two transformers - one three winding and one two winding;
  4. Two loads - 300/100 MW/Mvar and 240/80 MW/Mvar;
  5. External system which is system swing machine.

For transient stability calculation, three phase short circuit is simulated on the one of the lines between nodes Node 1 and Node 2. Fault occurs at t=1 s and is cleared at t=1.15 s by disconnecting the faulted line.
