Five Node Model

The model contains five nodes, two synchronous salient-pole (hydro) generators the rated voltage of which is 10 kV, three lines, two transformers, and single composite load. The composite load comprises three components: 1) constant power - 90 MW and 30 Mvar, 2) constant impedance - 30 MW and 10 Mvar, and 3) constant current - 30 MW and 10 Mvar. One of the generators is system swing machine. Single line diagram with power flow calculation results is given in the next figure.


For transient stability calculation, three phase short circuit is simulated on the one of the lines between nodes Node 1 and Node 2. Fault occurs at t=1 s and is cleared at t=1.15 s by disconnecting the faulted line.
