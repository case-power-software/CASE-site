Short Circuits

Short circuit calculation is a system calculation used for equipment dimensioning and protection adjustment. There are two types of short circuit calculation:

  1. Complete - Results of load flow calcultion, i.e. calculated voltages, are taken into account.
  2. Flat - All node voltages are assumed to be 1.0 pu. The method is widely used and follows IEC 60909â€‘0.

CASE supports short-circuit simulations at buses and lines. There are four types of short-circuits:

  1. Three phase
  2. Single phase to ground
  3. Two phases and
  4. Two phases to ground
